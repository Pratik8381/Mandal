About Us

A community organization dedicated to preserving traditions and spreading joy through Ganesh festivities in Tandulwadi Village

Our Mission

To organize magnificent Ganesh festivals that bring communities together, preserve our cultural heritage, and spread the divine blessings of Lord Ganesha to all devotees across all communities in Tandulwadi Village and surrounding areas.
